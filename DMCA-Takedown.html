DMCA Takedowns

If You Would Like To Take Down A Game That Is Yours Please Follow These Steps.

Most of these games are typically added in response to user requests, which means, it can make it challenging to ascertain their copyright status. If you suspect that a particular game contains copyrighted and protected content, we are committed to promptly removing it from our website and ensuring its removal from our Google searches.

Contact Information

Please contact us at: Rockygamesdevteam@gmail.com

To initiate the removal process for copyrighted content, please contact us via a corporate email address.

Why Corporate Email?

✅ Preventing False Copyright Claims: By using corporate email addresses, we can verify the authenticity of copyright claims, mitigating the risk of false reports.

✅ Swift Resolution: Our goal is to remove copyrighted content within 24 hours of receiving a copyright takedown email, ensuring a swift resolution for copyright holders.

✅ Support for Game Owners: This process guarantees that we honor only legitimate claims from official game owners.

Our primary objective is to maintain the integrity of copyright protection. When you reach out to us through a corporate email, we can swiftly act to remove the content from Google and our site.
